This is Erik de Castro Lopo bindings to it's own libsndfile, maintained by Philippe Strauss.

Bigarray unfinished experiments are in the ba/ subdir. Older Makefile in mk/



INSTALLATION:
	ocaml setup.ml -configure; ocaml setup.ml -build; ocaml setup.ml -install
	take a look at _oasis in case something is missing, you need oasis to regenerate ocamlbuild target.
